Virtual Association Summit by Electronics Socity (VASES) 2022

The Department of Electronics and Communication Engineering, Motilal Nehru National Institute of Technology Allahabad, proudly announces the hosting of International Conference on Optical,VLSI, Communication and Digital Signal Processing. The conference aims to provide a platform for academicians, individuals working in the industry, as well as students to showcase their research innovations and get recognition. VASES 2022 aspires to bring together research professional fraternity for the discussions and exchange of information on advances in Optical,VLSI, Communications and Digital Signal Processing. The scope of the conference embraces all the latest research trends in Electronics and Communication Engineering.
